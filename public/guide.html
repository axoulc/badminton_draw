Tournament Mode (Singles/Doubles)

üìç Settings ‚Üí Tournament Mode Section (Top)
  1. Click the Settings (‚öôÔ∏è) icon in the top-right header
  2. Look at the FIRST section called "Tournament Mode"
  3. You'll see two radio buttons:
    • Singles (1 vs 1)
    • Doubles (2 vs 2) - Partners rotate each round
  4. Select your preferred mode
  5. Click "Save Settings" at the bottom
Doubles Mode: Each player will automatically get a different partner in every round!

Score Matches (Fixed)

üìç Scoring Tab
  1. Click the "Scoring" tab in the navigation
  2. You'll see "Pending Matches" section
  3. Each match card shows:
    • Player Names / Player Names
    • A "Score Match" button
  4. Click "Score Match"
  5. Select which pair won
  6. Confirm the result

Reset Match Status

üìç Scoring Tab ‚Üí Completed Matches
  1. Go to Scoring tab
  2. Scroll to "Completed Matches" section
  3. Each completed match has a "Reset" button
  4. Click "Reset" to move match back to pending
  5. Player scores will automatically revert

Delete Rounds

üìç Rounds Tab ‚Üí Rounds History
  1. Click the "Rounds" tab
  2. Scroll to "Rounds History" section
  3. Each round card has a trash/delete icon (üóëÔ∏è) button
  4. Click the delete icon
  5. Confirm deletion (warning dialog)
  6. The round and all its scores will be removed
Warning: Deleting a round cannot be undone! All player scores from that round will be reverted.
